# ✨ Tales x Bhavya
Welcome to Tales x Bhavya — a personal blog and digital journal where I share my experiences, reflections, and learnings across life, tech, and self-growth. Whether it's a raw thought, a research insight, or a lesson from the journey, every post is a piece of my story.
---
## 📝 What's Inside?
- 📖 Blog Posts – Personal essays, thoughts, and everyday stories
- 🔬 Research Talks – Notes from discussions, articles, and experiments
- 💡 Ideas & Learnings – Tech insights, motivation, and mental models
- 🧠 Reflections – Realizations that shape my thinking and growth
---
## 🚀 Tech Stack
- HTML5 & CSS3
- Minimal custom styling for clean UI
- Hosted on [Vercel](https://vercel.com/)
