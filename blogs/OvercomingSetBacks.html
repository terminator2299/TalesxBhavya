Overcoming Setbacks

12th May, 2025

1. Past Rejections

Here is a list of my past rejections from various companies:

  • Failed Juspay
  • Failed Accenture
  • Failed Capgemini
  • Failed TechM
  • Failed HCL tech
  • Failed TCS
  • Failed Aptus Data Lab
  • Prp Webs
  • Failed MultiLipi
  • Failed Hp
  • ...and many more.

2. Current Situation

For the last 11 months, I have only gotten few opportunity for a technical interview, as I’ve been eliminated in the first round each time. Despite deploying projects and completing an internship in Machine Learning, I am learning MERN, iOS, and improving my DSA skills, I still struggle with solving questions in online assessments (OAs).

3. Mental and Emotional Struggles

It’s been hard to stay motivated after each rejection. But, I’ve been telling myself that my bad time will pass, especially with the arrival of 2025. I’ve been waiting for the right time to come, and even though I’ve been knocked down, my inner self and my father continue to push me to hustle, study harder, focus more on DSA, and improve my GitHub profile.

4. The Road Ahead

Even though I’ve been knocked down, I’ve never lost my drive. I keep pushing forward, and I believe the right opportunity will come when the time is right. I’m working on improving myself every day. I know that every bit of effort counts toward my growth, and eventually, I’ll make it.
